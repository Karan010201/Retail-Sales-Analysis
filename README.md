# Retail Sales Analysis & Business Insights

## 📌 Problem Statement

The objective of this project is to analyze retail sales data to identify key trends, top-performing regions, categories, and products, and provide actionable business insights.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn
* Jupyter Notebook

---

## 📊 Key Analysis Performed

* Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
* Sales Analysis by Region, Category, and Sub-Category
* Monthly Sales Trend Analysis
* Top 10 Products by Sales

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* West region generates the highest sales (~710K), indicating strong market demand.
* Technology category contributes the most to total revenue.
* Sub-categories like Phones and Chairs perform strongly, while Fasteners and Labels underperform.
* Sales peak during November–December, showing seasonal demand.
* A small number of products contribute significantly to total sales.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Focus marketing efforts in high-performing regions
* Invest more in top-selling categories and products
* Improve or reconsider low-performing sub-categories
* Plan seasonal campaigns based on sales trends
* Maintain inventory for high-demand products

Superstore Sales Dataset (Kaggle)
